Back Bar Grill menu is designed with consideration to locality, flavor, simplicity and authenticity. The menu includes sandwiches, veggie burgers and all-natural beef served in our choice of house baked artisan buns with a selection of premium cheeses, toppings and campus-made sauces. Sides include fries, sweet potato fries, onion strings, cilantro slaw and a balsamic baby green salad. Students can also create a combo to include a side item and soft drink with their sandwiches.
