FAQ

Where do my donations go?

Blocks and DineX are used to purchase meals from on-campus locations and then taken to local charities to help the less fortunate.

When will my meal blocks/DineX be removed from my account?

Your donated meal blocks or DineX will be removed from your account within 2-3 days.

How can I check how many meal blocks or how much DineX I have left?

You can check your remaining blocks/DineX through SIO under your Meal Plan Assignment.
